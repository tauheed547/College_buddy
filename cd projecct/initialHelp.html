Welcome to the Help Section!

Your guide to using Tara and College Mantra effectively

Introduction

At SRIT, we understand how important it is to have seamless access to information. That's why we've introduced two powerful assistants to guide you through your journey. Whether you're a student, faculty member, or visitor, our virtual assistants—Tara and College Mantra—are here to make your experience more efficient, interactive, and enjoyable.

Tara – Your Virtual Voice Assistant

Tara is designed for interactive voice-based assistance, allowing you to stay on the go without needing to type. If you’re multitasking or just prefer hands-free interaction, Tara is the perfect choice for you!

Key Features of Tara:

  • Voice Responses: Ask Tara anything, and it will answer you verbally.
  • Instant Assistance: Get quick answers to your questions about locations, schedules, events, and more.
  • Hands-Free Interaction: Perfect for when you need help while multitasking.
  • Personalized Help: Tara adapts to your preferences over time to make your experience even better.

How to Use Tara:
Simply speak your query aloud:

  • “Open website of SRIT?”
  • “Can you open faculty profile?”
  • “Open student login page?”

College Mantra – Your Chat Assistant

College Mantra is the perfect choice for when you prefer to type your questions and get fast, precise answers. From accessing campus resources to staying updated with the latest events, College Mantra can do it all!

Key Features of College Mantra:

  • Text-Based Interaction: Type your questions and receive instant, accurate responses.
  • Quick Access to Resources: Get direct links to portals, academic schedules, club details, and more.
  • Event Updates: Stay in the loop with the latest college events and deadlines.
  • Student Services: Access important student services like fee payments, academic resources, and more with ease.

How to Use College Mantra:
Simply type your query or choose from available options:

  • “Find location of library?”
  • “Open Toastmasters club?”
  • “Can you open exam portal?”

How to Choose Between Tara and College Mantra?

Choose Tara when you need voice responses and hands-free assistance.
Choose College Mantra when you prefer to type for quick information and access to resources.

Need More Help?

If you ever have more questions or need assistance beyond what these assistants provide, don’t hesitate to reach out to our support team. Tara and College Mantra are here to guide you every step of the way, ensuring your college journey is as smooth and efficient as possible.

Thank you for using College Mantra and Tara. We’re excited to help you navigate and succeed at SRIT. Let’s get started—your virtual assistant is just a click or a voice command away!
